FAQ

Do you do home delivery?

No, not at the moment. Pick up only.

Can we order online?

Sorry, we are not available on any online ordering services. Please call us on 02 4647 4256 to order.

Can you do gluten free meals?

Yes only, Oyster, Garlic Pepper and Cashew Nut stirfries, Pad Thai, Pad See Ew and Fried Rice can be made gluten free and only with Chicken, Seafood, Prawn or Vegtables.

Are you BYO?

Yes we are, BYO and fully licenced - we have house wine and beer.
